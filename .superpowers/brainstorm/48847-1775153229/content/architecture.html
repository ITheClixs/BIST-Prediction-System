Key Design Decisions

  • SQLite — zero-config, single file, perfect for local CLI tool
  • Rust via PyO3 — computing 50+ indicators × 100 stocks × years of history needs speed
  • Multi-source with fallback — Is Yatirim primary, Yahoo Finance backup, TCMB for macro
  • Model registry — track trained models, compare versions, roll back
  • Ensemble combiner — weighted average of XGBoost + LightGBM + LSTM + Transformer
